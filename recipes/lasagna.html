Lasagna

This is a tasty piece of Lasagna - yum! Full of tomatoes, cheest, and pasta. What is not to love?

First you take the pasta out, and then you make the pasta. then you add the cheese, then you add the tomatoes, then you put in the oven. Tadahh! you have cooked de lasagna!!

Ingredients

  • tomatoes
  • pasta
  • meat

Steps

  1. cut the tomatoes
  2. cook the meat
  3. add the tomatoes to the meat
  4. heat the water and put the pasta in
  5. take out pasta
  6. combine all Ingredients and put into the oven
  7. cook on 400 degrees F for 30 minutes
  8. Done!
